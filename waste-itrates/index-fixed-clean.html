About CAD Foundation

Pioneering cardiac care accessibility through innovative technology and community-driven healthcare solutions.

Our Mission

To create a cardiac disease-free society by making quality cardiac diagnosis accessible to every individual, regardless of their geographical location or economic status.

Founded by Dr. Padmanabha Kamath, CAD Foundation has transformed cardiac care delivery in Karnataka through innovative technology deployment and community health initiatives.

Our Services

Comprehensive cardiac care solutions delivered with precision and compassion.

ECG Testing

State-of-the-art electrocardiogram testing with instant results and expert analysis available at your doorstep across Karnataka.

Cardiac Consultation

Expert cardiac consultation by qualified cardiologists with personalized treatment plans and continuous monitoring support.

Emergency Care

24/7 emergency cardiac care services with rapid response teams and advanced life support equipment.

Health Monitoring

Continuous health monitoring programs with digital health records and preventive care recommendations.

Health Education

Community health education programs focusing on cardiac disease prevention and lifestyle modification.

Telemedicine

Advanced telemedicine platform connecting rural patients with specialists through secure video consultations.
