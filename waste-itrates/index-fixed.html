Our Impact

We are the change makers of 2021 with more than 325 installations across 23 districts in Karnataka and uniting 2500+ doctors in Pan India Project.

How We Achieved Our Mission

Our journey from losing a young patient to building a network of cardiac care across Karnataka

2014: The Beginning

After losing a young patient to a massive heart attack due to delayed diagnosis, started collecting demographic data about rural healthcare needs.

2015-2017: Research & Data

Collected data about villages/towns, nearest hospitals, PHCs and local difficulties. Maintained a database of doctors in rural areas.

2018: Digital Revolution

Formed a WhatsApp group of vital doctors in Feb 2018 â€” a turning point; educated them digitally and conducted workshop 'Anveshana'.

Today: 325 ECG Machines

Dedicated 325 ECG machines to health centers in remote areas across 23 districts, serving over a crore beneficiaries.
