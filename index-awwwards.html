About CAD Foundation

Building a Cardiac Disease Free Society by Helping the Underprivileged

CAD team achieved its historical mark of 325 ECG Machines installation in 23 districts of Karnataka. Unbelievable achievements for CAD team in its journey, where it created the ECG awareness program and installation in 23 districts and in 325 primary health care centers and local govt hospitals.

Healthcare Supply

Provide health care by supplying ECG machines & other ancillary machines to primary health centers

National Coverage

Cover all four corners of India with instant medical support

Community Education

Educate, train, create awareness among people, localities and rural communities

Massive Impact Goal

Our Journey

Timeline of Excellence

From a heartbreaking loss to transforming cardiac care across Karnataka

2018

The Beginning

After losing a young patient to a massive heart attack due to delayed diagnosis, started collecting demographic data about rural healthcare needs.

Research

Data Collection Phase

Collected data about villages/towns, nearest hospitals, PHCs and local difficulties. Maintained a database of doctors in rural areas.
